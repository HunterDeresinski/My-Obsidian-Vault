## <u>Euclideon Algorithm</u>
Method for finding gcd(a,b)

## Example
GCD(414, 662) = 2
662 = 1 * 414 + 248
414 = 1 * 248 + 166
248 = 1 * 166 + 82
166 = 2 * 82 + 2
82 = 41 * 2 + 0
